About Us

Who We Are

Fashio is an emerging Pakistani fashion brand offering high-quality, trending products at affordable prices. We deal in fancy, casual, and party wear dresses, both in retail and wholesale. Our wholesale prices are significantly lower, providing excellent value for bulk purchases. With over 25 years of experience in this industry, we invite you to visit our shop at City Center, Bank Road, Saddar Rawalpindi.

Our Team

Our dedicated team at Fashio is a blend of seasoned professionals and passionate newcomers, all united by a love for fashion and a commitment to quality. With decades of collective experience, our designers, tailors, and customer service experts work tirelessly to bring you the finest garments. Each team member plays a crucial role in ensuring that every piece meets our high standards, delivering exceptional style and value to our customers.
